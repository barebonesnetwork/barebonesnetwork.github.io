About Us

Our goal is to try to archive our creations and the creations of others. We are doing this through the use of internet archive tools,
such as the Wayback Machine, which you can find here, as well as random stuff that we made from our computers.
Our site will contain everything from chatrooms to browser versions of Minecraft. We hope that you enjoy the stuff that you find!
Thank you for coming along for the ride. We appreciate it.
